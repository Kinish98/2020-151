Heart Disease Risk Prediction

This is the main function of the mobile software solution. The user has to answer for a questionnaire series. The questionnaire series is based on the user's family history, substance abuses, smoking details and other diseases. The questionnaire series is reviewed and verified by the cardiologist consultant at Kandy General Hospital.

Based on the user's details, the software solution will provide a heart disease risk level percentage for the user. By using machine learning technologies, the mobile application has the ability to predict the weather the person is having a heart disease risk or not. questionnaire series is listed in the below section.

  • Are you already diagnosed as having heart disease?
  • Are you a smoker?
  • Do you have a history of substance abuse?
  • Do you suffer from high blood pressure?
  • Do you have a family history of heart disease?
  • Do you have hyperlipidemia?
  • Do you have a history of excessive use of alcohol or caffeine?
  • Do you engage in regular exercises?
  • Do you have any chest pain, neck, back, or jaw pain on exertion?
  • Do you have sudden cold sweat?
  • Do you feel any Dizzy spells?

Using the latest technologies in an efficient, productive, and influenced manner to facilitate a mobile application that is used to produce a Heart disease risk level for the user. For the disease prediction functionality, the systerm used mainly five machine learning algorithms to get the best accurate algorithm to predict the risk level. The Used algorithms are listed below.

  • K nearest Neighbors algorithm
  • Support Vector Machine
  • Bagging Algorithm
  • Random Forest Algorithm
  • ET Classifier Algorithm
